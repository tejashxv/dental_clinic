Our Specialization

RCT & Re-RCT Specialist

Root canal treatment (RCT) is one of the most technique-sensitive procedures in dentistry — and it's the one we've spent over 14+ years perfecting. Beyond routine root canals, Dr. Vaishnavi specializes in Re-RCT (root canal retreatment) for teeth where a previous root canal has failed, leaving patients with lingering pain, swelling or infection.

Using rotary endodontic instruments, detailed diagnostic imaging and a magnification-assisted approach, we pinpoint exactly why a tooth is still troubling you and retreat it with precision — giving it a genuine second chance instead of moving straight to extraction.

Painless RCT with modern rotary technique Expert Re-RCT for failed or reinfected root canals Single-visit RCT for eligible cases Focused on saving your natural tooth, not extracting it
